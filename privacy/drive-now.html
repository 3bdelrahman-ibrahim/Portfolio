IMPORTANT PRIVACY NOTICE

Before using Drive Now, please read this important information about how we collect and use your data:

  • User Information: We collect your name, contact details, and ride preferences.
  • Location Data: We collect precise GPS data to provide location-based services like ride pickup and drop-off.
  • Background Location Access: We may collect location data even when the app is closed or not in use. This is necessary to support live ride tracking and enhance user and driver safety.
  • Driver and Vehicle Data: For drivers, we collect vehicle details, licenses, and document photos.

Why do we collect background location?

Drive Now requires background location access to enable accurate tracking during trips, even when the app is in the background. This is essential for pickup/drop-off, driver navigation, and emergency safety measures. Without it, the app cannot perform its core functionality.
