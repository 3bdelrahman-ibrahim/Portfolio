IMPORTANT PRIVACY NOTICE

Before using Salam Space, please read this important information about how we collect and use your data:

  • User Information: We collect your name, contact details, and app preferences.
  • Precise Location Data: We collect precise GPS location data to provide location-based services such as finding nearby mosques, prayer times, and Qibla direction. This includes both foreground and background location access.
  • Background Location Access: We collect precise location data even when the app is closed or not in use. This is necessary to provide accurate prayer time notifications and Qibla direction updates based on your location.
  • Device Information: We collect device data for app performance monitoring and to provide a better user experience.

Why do we collect precise location data?

Salam Space requires precise location access to provide essential Islamic services including:

  • Accurate prayer times based on your exact location
  • Finding nearby mosques and Islamic centers
  • Providing accurate Qibla direction
  • Location-based Islamic event notifications

Without precise location access, these core features of the app cannot function properly.
