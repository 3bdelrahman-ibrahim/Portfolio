IMPORTANT PRIVACY NOTICE

At Salam Space, we value your privacy. Here's what you need to know about how we handle your data:

  • Location Data Only: We only use your device's location to provide accurate Qibla direction and prayer times. This data is processed locally on your device and is never stored or shared.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our priority.
  • Local Processing: All calculations for Qibla direction and prayer times are done on your device. No data is sent to our servers.
  • No Account Required: You can use all features of Salam Space without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Why do we need location access?

Salam Space requires location access solely to calculate accurate Qibla direction and prayer times based on your current position. This is essential for the core functionality of the app. Your location is used only when the app is active and is never stored or transmitted.
