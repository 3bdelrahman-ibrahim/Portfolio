Privacy Policy

IMPORTANT PRIVACY NOTICE

Before using Fahs Elriadah, please read this important information about how we collect and use your data:

  • Vehicle Data: We collect vehicle diagnostic data via OBD (On-Board Diagnostics) when you connect to your car. This may include error codes, sensor data, and technical information.
  • Device Information: We collect device model, operating system, and unique identifiers to ensure compatibility and improve performance.
  • Bluetooth Access: The app requires Bluetooth to connect to your OBD device and retrieve vehicle data.
  • Location Data: We may collect location data to enhance certain features, such as mapping or trip logging, but only when necessary and with your permission.

Why do we collect OBD and device data?

Fahs Elriadah requires access to your vehicle's OBD data and device information to provide accurate diagnostics, display real-time vehicle status, and help you maintain your car's health. Without this access, the app cannot perform its core functionality.

Required App Permissions and Their Usage

Permission Purpose Data Usage
Bluetooth Connect to OBD device Used to establish a connection and retrieve vehicle diagnostic data
Location (if enabled) Enhance features like trip logging or mapping Used only when required for specific features and with user consent
Storage Save diagnostic reports and app data Stores vehicle reports, logs, and user preferences
Notifications App alerts and updates Used to notify users about important vehicle issues or app updates

2. Information We Collect

  • Vehicle diagnostic data (error codes, sensor readings, status reports) via OBD connection
  • Device information (model, OS version, unique identifiers)
  • Bluetooth connection data
  • Location data (if enabled by user)
  • App usage data for analytics and improvement
  • Diagnostic reports and logs saved on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real-time vehicle diagnostics and status updates
  • To generate and store vehicle health reports
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To notify you about important vehicle issues or app updates
  • To enhance features such as trip logging or mapping (if location is enabled)

4. Data Security

We use encryption, secure storage, and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your diagnostic data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share it.

5. User Control & Rights

  • You can access and delete diagnostic reports stored on your device at any time.
  • You can revoke Bluetooth and location access through your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of any data you have shared with us via support.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for app operation or as required by law. Diagnostic data is stored locally unless you choose to share it.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.
